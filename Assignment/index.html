Jollof Rice

A popular Nigerian rice meal cooked with tomato stew.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Rice (2 cups)
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Oil
  • Seasoning & Spices

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, pepper and onions.
  2. Heat oil, fry tomato paste a little.
  3. Add the blended mix and spices, cook it.
  4. Put the washed rice into the pot and stir.
  5. Add water and allow to cook on low heat.
  6. Reduce the heat and let it steam for a while.

Tips

For a party taste, let the bottom burn a little (not too much).
